My Life Story

Growing up in the serene valleys of Uttarakhand, my life has been a rich blend of simple village values, athletic determination, technological exploration, and an unyielding passion for nature's grandest peaks.

I was born and raised in Bailparao, a picturesque village nestled in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, into a traditional Kumaoni family. Educated in local government schools, my childhood years were defined by a close connection to the land and a vibrant, active lifestyle. In my early years, I was exceptionally active in sports, especially cricket and running. My friends and I spent our days exploring the outdoors, playing games like Chor-Police, going hunting in the nearby jungles, and participating in tactical village mock-fighting games where two neighboring villages would test their strategy and teamwork in friendly battles.

After completing my 12th grade, I pursued my higher studies by earning a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) degree from PNG PG College in Ramnagar. Driven by a growing interest in computer systems and software, I then moved to Bhimtal to pursue a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at the Birla Institute of Applied Sciences. This academic foundation culminated in a successful campus placement, launching me directly into my professional career in software engineering.

It was during my professional career as a software engineer that I discovered a deep passion for trekking, which quickly evolved into high-altitude mountaineering. The challenges of the office were matched by the physical and mental demands of the wild. To date, I have climbed two challenging 6,000+ meter mountains in the Ladakh region: Kiagar Ri North Face (6,100 mt) and Peak P6250 (6,250 mt). Driven by the call of the high peaks, my goal is to climb peaks higher than 7,000+ meters in the future.

Alongside my career in technology and my pursuit of high summits, I have developed a strong passion for farming. I actively practice agriculture alongside my daily work as a software engineer, finding a unique, meditative balance between writing code and working with the earth. This lifestyle allows me to stay grounded to my roots in Uttarakhand while building software that shapes the future.
